Software Development

Mobile Application Development

Software Testing

Jeka Innovations is a company that provides software testing services at all levels, from manual to automated tests. Cloud and DevOps Services

Jeka Innovations delivers full-cycle cloud and DevOps services to empower your business for continuous integration and deployment.
